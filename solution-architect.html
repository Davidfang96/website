SOLUTIONS ARCHITECT / SYSTEMS INTEGRATOR
Position
1733
Job Category
Information Systems Consulting
Systems Architecture

US - California
Full or Part Time
Full-Time
Key Responsibilities
The Solutions Architect/Systems Integrator will work directly with our clients to assess their information technology needs and architect world class enterprise solutions. Responsibilities include business development and other pre-sales activities (e.g., technical and price proposals) down through actual project execution. The Solutions Architect will provide technical leadership and oversight of design and development staff and be "hands on" during the full systems development life cycle.
Critical Technical & Behavioral Skills/Qualifications

The ideal candidate will have, at a minimum, a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (or related field) and 12 years of relevant professional experience in the following areas: systems integration and application architecture, "build versus buy" recommendations, alternatives analysis, Web-enabled architectures, middleware, visual modeling, object-oriented design, and full life cycle development of ERP & CRM enterprise applications. The candidate must have extensive experience with Web-based technologies and services including J2EE (JSPs, EJBs, JDBC) and/or .NET (C#, ASP.NET, ADO.NET), Web servers (Apache, IIS), application servers (Oracle 9iAS/10gAS, WebLogic, WebSphere, IIS), and relational databases (Oracle 9i/10g, SQL Server). Knowledge of design patterns is required and experience with the Rational Unified Process (RUP) tools and the Software Engineering Institute's Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI) is a big plus.

The position requires outstanding written and verbal communications skills as the candidate will interact with clients and senior management. Project planning and estimating skills are required to ensure the successful completion of projects. Previous off-shore across-time-zone collaboration and management experience is a plus.

Travel Requirements
25 – 50%
