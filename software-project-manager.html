Software Project Manager

Title
Project Manager
Position
1627
Job Category
Programming/Development Project Management
Location
US - California
Full or Part Time
Full-Time
Key Responsibilities
This position will be responsible for the oversight and coordination of technical projects and teams to include; Database Administrators, Developers, Release Manager, Configuration Management, etc. Provide the technical contact point to customer, partner and management. Responsibilities to include;
-Communicate technical situations and incidents during briefings
-Attend all technical review boards.
-Optimize implementation technical processes.
-Oversee documentation of procedures and policies.
-Provide administrative support to the technical team (DBAs and Developers).
-Lead international technical projects as assigned.
-Work with customer management on proposal and service request efforts.
-Participate in technical discussion and coordinate resources and resolution.
Critical Technical & Behavioral Skills/Qualifications

B.S./B.A. or equivalent work experience. 10 years of management or lead experience. 9 years of more of hands on experience with Oracle products and technology. 4 or more years of Program and Project management experience.

Combination of two or more of the following Oracle products experience; 7i/8i/9i/a0g DBA with WMS and RAC, Sensor Edge Server, Single Sign On, Windows NT, SQL Server. Outstanding written and verbal skills are required to interact with clients, senior management and developers.

Prior international work experience is highly desirable.

Travel Requirements
25 – 50 %
